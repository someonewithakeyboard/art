Tagline

Art theory can be quite boring, considering the subject is supposed to be a colourful and imaginiative subject, and while theory is important, it ends up being boring. Our group has decided to make an incentive to do amazing theory work, and to outperform their peers.

Our point system gives rewards to superb work and top-notch behaviour within the classroom, and at the end of the year, students have the oppotunity to sell it off for 3D-printed prizes that they can paint, to remember Art throughout their time at Cecil Hills High School

A look at some of the prizes:

These 3D prints can be painted, so you can go all out with the colours

These 3D prints have a ring, so you can hang it up on a keychain

They're easy to print, meaning that you can make them on a whim
